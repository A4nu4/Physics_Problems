To find the magnetic field at the midpoint between two parallel wires carrying opposite currents, we use the principle of superposition and the formula for the magnetic field produced by a long, straight wire.

### Given Starting Values:
*   **Current in Wire 1 ($I_1$):** $5 \text{ A}$ (e.g., directed into the page)
*   **Current in Wire 2 ($I_2$):** $5 \text{ A}$ (directed out of the page)
*   **Separation ($d$):** $10 \text{ cm} = 0.1 \text{ m}$
*   **Distance to midpoint ($r$):** $d/2 = 0.05 \text{ m}$
*   **Permeability of free space ($\mu_0$):** $4\pi \times 10^{-7} \text{ T}\cdot\text{m/A}$

---

### 1. Determine the Direction of the Fields
Using the **Right-Hand Rule** for a wire:
*   **Wire 1 (Current into page):** The magnetic field lines wrap clockwise. At the midpoint (to the right of Wire 1), the field vector $B_1$ points **downward**.
*   **Wire 2 (Current out of page):** The magnetic field lines wrap counter-clockwise. At the midpoint (to the left of Wire 2), the field vector $B_2$ also points **downward**.

Since both vectors point in the same direction, they will add together.

---

### 2. Calculate Individual Field Magnitudes
The magnitude of the magnetic field at a distance $r$ from a long wire is given by:

$$B = \frac{\mu_0 I}{2\pi r}$$

For Wire 1:
$$B_1 = \frac{(4\pi \times 10^{-7}) \cdot 5}{2\pi \cdot 0.05} = \frac{2 \times 10^{-7} \cdot 5}{0.05}$$
$$B_1 = 2 \times 10^{-5} \text{ T}$$

Since the current and distance are the same for Wire 2, $B_2 = 2 \times 10^{-5} \text{ T}$.

---

### 3. Calculate the Net Magnetic Field ($B_{net}$)
$$B_{net} = B_1 + B_2$$
$$B_{net} = 2 \times 10^{-5} \text{ T} + 2 \times 10^{-5} \text{ T}$$
$$B_{net} = 4 \times 10^{-5} \text{ T}$$

---

### Final Result:
The magnitude of the magnetic field at the midpoint is **$4 \times 10^{-5} \text{ T}$** (or $40 \ \mu\text{T}$). The direction is **perpendicular to the plane** containing the wires, determined by the Right-Hand Rule (in this example, "downward" relative to the wire orientations).
